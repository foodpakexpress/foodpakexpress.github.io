Your one stop shop About Food-Pak Express

Food-Pak Express is a food wholesale company in the metropolitan Boston area. Easily accessible from the Massachusetts Turnpike and Expressway, Food-Pak Express deals with the wholesale of food products, ingredients and equipment. Our inventory covers the needs of any restaurant establishment from A-Z, with products from all over the world, from spices to canned goods, from pastas to oils, from meat to poultry, from fruits to vegetables, from paper to packaging, from water to cocktails, juices and bar needs.

Food-Pak Express is well-equipped to fill the needs of any kitchen with quantities ranging from a piece, to a case, to a pallet, even up to an entire truckload. It is our goal to personally provide quality service and products to restaurants of all types of ethnicities. We have products to specifically address the unique needs of pizzerias, sub shops, seafood establishments, delis, Chinese, Japanese, Indian, Greek, Italian, American, Thai and Korean establishments, catering companies, bakery, donut and pastry shops, fast food operations, even canteen trucks with a variety of products of prepared, fresh and frozen items, paper and packaging, dry goods and consumables.

With over 40,000 square feet of dry goods and ingredients floor space, 30,000 square feet of paper and packaging floor space and over 8,000 square feet of refrigeration and freezer floor space, we have all of your needs under one roof. We have a fully-stocked kitchen and restaurant equipment showroom. We carry a complete line of catering products, thousands of choices, sizes, colors, quantities and specialties. We receive fresh produce daily. Our refrigerator and cooler spaces are among the largest in the state and are packed with all you need. We have a meat and poultry department that you have to see to believe. Our freezer department carries a wide variety of products. If we don't carry every product you need, we'll bring it in for you. We are a one stop shop.

We cater to you, the restaurant owner, the catering company, the small market or the grocery store. We have a fully stocked kitchen equipment and restaurant furniture and fixture department inside. We have all the large and small equipment you need, all the small wares for your business. We provide excellent service. We pride ourselves on serving you with the most professional attitude and assistance available. Most importantly, we strive to bring you the lowest prices around. We are sure that once you shop at Food-pak Express, you will be so impressed with our quality of merchandise and our extremely low prices, that you will be a treasured customer for years to come.

Note: Food-Pak Express is a members only wholesale store. Members are required to show proof of business by submitting a copy of their Federal Tax ID. If you have any questions or concerns please contact us.
